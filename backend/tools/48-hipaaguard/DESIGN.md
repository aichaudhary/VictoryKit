# HIPAAGuard Design Document

## Tool Overview
**Tool Name:** HIPAAGuard  
**Tool Number:** 48  
**Purpose:** HIPAA Compliance Management & Healthcare Data Protection  
**Domain:** hipaaguard.maula.ai  
**Ports:** 3048 (Frontend), 4048 (API), 6048 (WebSocket), 8048 (ML)  
**Database:** hipaaguard_db

## Core Functionality

### 1. Risk Assessment Management
- **Comprehensive Security Assessments:** Evaluate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
- **Rule Compliance Tracking:** Monitor compliance with Privacy, Security, Breach Notification, and Enforcement rules
- **Risk Scoring:** Calculate overall risk scores and safeguard-specific scores (0-100)
- **Assessment Types:** Initial, annual, incident-triggered, and pre-audit assessments
- **Finding Management:** Track critical, high, medium, and low severity findings with status tracking
- **Approval Workflow:** Assessor approval process with scheduled review cycles

### 2. PHI Discovery & Data Mapping
- **Automated PHI Scanning:** Discover Protected Health Information across systems, databases, file shares, and applications
- **PHI Type Detection:** Identify names, SSN, MRN, DOB, addresses, phone, email, insurance, diagnosis, and treatment data
- **Location Tracking:** Map PHI storage locations with encryption and access control status
- **Risk Classification:** Categorize locations by risk level (critical, high, medium, low)
- **Scan Types:** Support full, targeted, and incremental scanning strategies
- **Statistics Dashboard:** Track encrypted vs. unencrypted locations and critical findings

### 3. Breach Incident Management
- **Incident Tracking:** Record breach incidents with incident, discovery, and reported dates
- **Breach Classification:** Categorize by type (unauthorized access, theft, loss, improper disposal, hacking, ransomware, insider, other)
- **Affected Individuals:** Track count and types of PHI compromised
- **Notification Workflow:** Manage individual, HHS, and media notifications
- **Investigation Management:** Document root cause analysis and remediation steps
- **Status Tracking:** Monitor breach lifecycle from identification through resolution
- **Compliance Reporting:** Generate breach summaries for regulatory reporting

### 4. Business Associate Agreements (BAA)
- **Agreement Management:** Track BAAs and subcontractor agreements
- **PHI Access Control:** Define full, limited, or no PHI access levels
- **Lifecycle Tracking:** Monitor draft, pending signature, active, expired, and terminated statuses
- **Expiration Alerts:** Proactive notifications for expiring agreements (90-day default)
- **Audit Schedule:** Track last audit date and schedule next audits
- **Compliance Scoring:** Measure business associate compliance (0-100)
- **Violation Tracking:** Record violations with severity levels and resolution status
- **Document Management:** Store and track agreement documents

### 5. Employee Training Management
- **Course Types:** Initial HIPAA, annual refresher, privacy rule, security rule, breach notification, role-specific, and incident response
- **Employee Tracking:** Monitor training by employee ID, name, role, and department
- **Completion Management:** Track training dates, completion dates, and expiration dates
- **Certification:** Store certificate URLs and verify passing scores (default 80%)
- **Automated Reminders:** Send initial, reminder, and final notifications
- **Overdue Monitoring:** Track expired and overdue training records
- **Training Statistics:** Calculate completion rates and average scores

### 6. Access Audit Logs
- **Comprehensive Logging:** Record all PHI access events with timestamps
- **Action Tracking:** Monitor view, create, update, delete, export, print, and share actions
- **User Attribution:** Link access to user ID, name, role, and location
- **Resource Tracking:** Log patient records, PHI, reports, and system access
- **Suspicious Activity Detection:** Flag unusual access patterns with reasoning
- **Minimum Necessary:** Track compliance with minimum necessary standard
- **Access Controls:** Record granted and denied access with denial reasons
- **Audit Analytics:** Generate access statistics and suspicious activity reports

### 7. Compliance Reporting
- **Report Types:** Risk assessments, audits, breach summaries, annual reviews, OCR requests, and custom reports
- **Coverage Periods:** Define report start and end dates
- **Status Workflow:** Draft, finalized, and submitted states
- **Compliance Scoring:** Overall compliance percentage (0-100)
- **Finding Summary:** Track total, critical, high, medium, and low findings
- **Recommendations:** Generate actionable remediation recommendations
- **Approval Chain:** Track generated by, reviewed by, and approved by
- **Submission Tracking:** Record submitted to and submission dates

## AI Assistant Integration (Neural Link)

### Healthcare Compliance Assistant
- **Compliance Guidance:** Answer HIPAA questions and provide regulatory interpretation
- **Risk Analysis:** Evaluate potential compliance risks and recommend safeguards
- **Breach Assessment:** Analyze incidents and determine notification requirements
- **Policy Generation:** Create customized HIPAA policies and procedures
- **Training Content:** Generate training materials and quizzes
- **Audit Preparation:** Guide users through OCR audit preparation
- **Remediation Planning:** Suggest corrective actions for findings
- **Best Practices:** Recommend industry-standard security controls

### AI Functions
1. **Risk Scoring:** ML-based risk calculation using historical data
2. **PHI Pattern Detection:** Advanced pattern matching for PHI discovery
3. **Anomaly Detection:** Identify unusual access patterns in audit logs
4. **Breach Prediction:** Predict potential breach scenarios based on vulnerabilities
5. **Compliance Forecasting:** Predict compliance trends and recommend interventions
6. **Natural Language Queries:** "Show me all critical findings from last quarter"
7. **Smart Notifications:** Intelligent alert prioritization and routing
8. **Automated Reporting:** Generate compliance reports from natural language prompts
9. **Policy Recommendations:** Suggest policy updates based on regulatory changes
10. **Training Optimization:** Personalize training content based on role and performance

## Technical Architecture

### Backend API Endpoints

#### Risk Assessments
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/risk-assessments` - Create new assessment
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/risk-assessments` - List assessments (with filters)
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/risk-assessments/:id` - Get assessment details
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/risk-assessments/:id` - Update assessment
- `DELETE /api/v1/hipaaguard/risk-assessments/:id` - Delete assessment

#### PHI Discovery
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/phi-scans` - Initiate PHI scan
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/phi-scans` - List scans
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/phi-scans/:id` - Get scan results
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/phi-scans/:id` - Update scan

#### Breach Management
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/breaches` - Report breach incident
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/breaches` - List breaches
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/breaches/statistics` - Get breach statistics
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/breaches/:id` - Get breach details
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/breaches/:id` - Update breach

#### Business Associate Agreements
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/baas` - Create BAA
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/baas` - List BAAs
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/baas/expiring` - Get expiring BAAs
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/baas/:id` - Get BAA details
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/baas/:id` - Update BAA

#### Training Management
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings` - Create training record
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings` - List trainings
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings/overdue` - Get overdue trainings
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings/statistics` - Get training stats
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings/:id` - Get training details
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/trainings/:id` - Update training

#### Access Logs
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/access-logs` - Create access log
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/access-logs` - List access logs
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/access-logs/suspicious` - Get suspicious activity
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/access-logs/statistics` - Get access statistics
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/access-logs/:id` - Get log details

#### Compliance Reports
- `POST /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-reports` - Create report
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-reports` - List reports
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-reports/:id` - Get report details
- `PUT /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-reports/:id` - Update report
- `DELETE /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-reports/:id` - Delete report

#### Dashboard
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/dashboard` - Get dashboard data
- `GET /api/v1/hipaaguard/compliance-overview` - Get compliance overview

### Database Schema
- **RiskAssessment:** Comprehensive risk assessment records with safeguard and rule compliance scores
- **PHIDiscovery:** PHI scanning results with location mapping and encryption status
- **Breach:** Breach incident tracking with notification workflow and remediation
- **BAA:** Business associate agreement management with lifecycle tracking
- **Training:** Employee training records with completion and expiration tracking
- **AccessLog:** Comprehensive access audit logs with suspicious activity flagging
- **ComplianceReport:** Compliance report generation and submission tracking

### Security Considerations
- **Audit Trail:** All operations logged for compliance and forensics
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions (HIPAA Security Officer, Privacy Officer, Auditor, User)
- **Data Encryption:** Sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Session Management:** Secure authentication with automatic timeout
- **Breach Notifications:** Automated compliance with breach notification rules
- **Data Retention:** Configurable retention policies meeting HIPAA requirements
